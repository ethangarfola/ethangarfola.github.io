Subclass: Revenant

Aspects: Shatter dive and Grim Harvest. Shatterdive, paired with the glacier grenade is fantastic for clearing adds

Fragments: Whisper of Fissures, Whisper of Chains, Whisper of Hedrons, Whisper of Shards, Whisper of Bonds

Throw down glacier grenade, then when everyone is frozen, shatterdive into it, killing all the adds. This will create stasis shards that you pick it up. Then you go around and use your finisher to kill everyone to keep you invisbile until you regenerate your glacier grenade and repeat the cycle.
