Subclass: Silkstike

Aspects: Whirling Maelstrom and Widow's Silk

Fragments: Thread of Ascent, Thread of Generation, Thread of Fury, Thread of Transmutation

Use grapple to recieve woven mail and reload all weapons. Use the grapple melee, and then get kills while you have woven mail to create a tangle. When the tangle is shot, the whirling maelstrom will be created and damage any surrounding enemies.
